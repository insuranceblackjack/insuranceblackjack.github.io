Top 10 Travel Insurance Policies for Couples Traveling Together

Home / Blog / Blog Details

Traveling as a couple is one of life’s greatest joys—whether it’s a romantic getaway, an adventurous backpacking trip, or a long-overdue honeymoon. However, unexpected events like flight cancellations, medical emergencies, or lost luggage can quickly turn a dream vacation into a nightmare. That’s why having the right travel insurance is crucial.

In today’s world, where geopolitical tensions, climate-related disruptions, and health concerns like lingering COVID-19 variants are ever-present, securing comprehensive travel insurance is more important than ever. Below, we’ve curated the top 10 travel insurance policies for couples traveling together, considering coverage, affordability, and real-world reliability.

1. World Nomads Explorer Plan

Best for Adventurous Couples

If you and your partner thrive on adrenaline-pumping activities like hiking, scuba diving, or skiing, World Nomads’ Explorer Plan is a top pick. It covers over 200 adventure sports and offers emergency medical evacuation—a must for remote destinations.

Key Features:

  • Coverage for extreme sports (e.g., bungee jumping, rock climbing)
  • 24/7 emergency assistance
  • Theft and baggage protection

Drawback: Slightly pricier than standard plans, but worth it for thrill-seekers.

2. Allianz Travel Insurance (OneTrip Premier)

Best for Frequent Travelers

Couples who travel multiple times a year should consider Allianz’s OneTrip Premier. It offers annual multi-trip coverage, eliminating the need to buy separate policies for each getaway.

Key Features:

  • Trip cancellation up to $100,000 per person
  • Emergency medical and dental coverage
  • Rental car damage protection

Bonus: Covers "cancel for any reason" (CFAR) with an upgrade.

3. Travelex Travel Select

Best for Budget-Conscious Couples

For couples who want solid coverage without breaking the bank, Travelex Travel Select strikes a great balance between affordability and protection.

Key Features:

  • Primary medical coverage (no need to file with personal insurance first)
  • 24-hour travel assistance
  • Covers trip interruptions due to weather or strikes

Note: Adventure sports require an add-on.

4. Seven Corners RoundTrip Elite

Best for International Travel

If you’re jetting off to Europe, Asia, or beyond, Seven Corners’ RoundTrip Elite provides robust international coverage, including COVID-19-related issues.

Key Features:

  • $1 million in emergency medical evacuation
  • Covers pandemic-related cancellations
  • Optional rental car collision

Ideal For: Couples visiting multiple countries in one trip.

5. IMG Patriot Platinum

Best for Long-Term Travelers

Planning a months-long sabbatical or round-the-world trip? IMG’s Patriot Platinum offers extended coverage, perfect for digital nomad couples.

Key Features:

  • Coverage for trips up to 12 months
  • Includes mental health benefits
  • Evacuation for political unrest

Downside: Not ideal for short weekend trips.

6. AXA Assistance USA (Gold Plan)

Best for Luxury Travelers

If you’re splurging on a five-star resort or a cruise, AXA’s Gold Plan ensures high-touch service and extensive cancellation coverage.

Key Features:

  • 150% reimbursement for trip interruptions
  • Missed connection coverage
  • Concierge service for last-minute bookings

Perk: Covers pre-existing conditions if purchased within 14 days of booking.

7. SafetyWing Nomad Insurance

Best for Digital Nomad Couples

For remote-working couples hopping between countries, SafetyWing offers flexible, subscription-based insurance with no fixed end date.

Key Features:

  • Auto-renews every 28 days
  • Covers COVID-19 like any other illness
  • Includes limited home country coverage

Limitation: Lower medical limits compared to traditional plans.

8. John Hancock Gold Plan

Best for Senior Couples

Older travelers often face higher premiums, but John Hancock’s Gold Plan offers competitive rates with senior-friendly benefits.

Key Features:

  • No age limit for coverage
  • Pre-existing condition waiver
  • Cancel-for-any-reason option

Why It’s Great: No medical questionnaire for travelers under 70.

9. Berkshire Hathaway Travel Protection (ExactCare Extra)

Best for Customizable Coverage

If you want to tailor your policy, Berkshire Hathaway’s ExactCare Extra lets you pick and choose add-ons like sports or electronics coverage.

Key Features:

  • "Cash back" feature for unused coverage
  • Covers lost or stolen gadgets
  • No deductibles for medical claims

Flexibility: Adjust coverage based on destination risks.

10. Generali Global Assistance (Premium Plan)

Best for All-Inclusive Protection

For couples who want zero hassle, Generali’s Premium Plan bundles strong medical, cancellation, and baggage protection into one seamless policy.

Key Features:

  • 175% reimbursement for trip interruptions
  • Free coverage for children under 17
  • 24/7 emergency support

Standout: Fast claims processing via mobile app.

Final Tips for Choosing Couple Travel Insurance

  • Compare deductibles and limits—some policies have hidden caps.
  • Check COVID-19 coverage—many insurers now treat it like any other illness.
  • Read reviews—real customer experiences reveal claim-handling efficiency.

Whether you’re backpacking through Southeast Asia or enjoying a vineyard tour in Italy, the right travel insurance ensures peace of mind. Safe travels!
