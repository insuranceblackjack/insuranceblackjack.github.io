The Rise of Neighborhood Electric Vehicles

In recent years, Neighborhood Electric Vehicles (NEVs), including golf carts, have surged in popularity across Georgia. From retirement communities in Peachtree City to beachside neighborhoods in Savannah, these compact, eco-friendly vehicles are redefining short-distance transportation. However, as their usage expands beyond golf courses, so does the need for proper Georgia golf cart insurance quotes to protect owners from unforeseen risks.

Why Golf Carts Are More Than Just Recreational Vehicles

Golf carts are no longer confined to country clubs. Many Georgia residents now use them for:
- Daily errands (grocery runs, school pickups)
- Community patrols (security, maintenance)
- Tourism and rentals (resorts, event venues)

With this shift, state regulations have tightened. Georgia law requires golf carts operating on public roads to meet specific safety standards, including:
- Headlights, taillights, and turn signals
- Seat belts
- Valid insurance coverage
